    About Us

    Eatler India Pvt Ltd is the world's first transparent kitchen brought at your doorstep. Eatler is an efficient food delivery app that not only provides you with food from your favorite restaurants but also gives you a 360 degree vision of what happens from the moment you crave your favorite food to the time you have it served on your platter. We are an Experience that provides you with Transparency and the best Quality.

    • Our mission is to become the consumer’s primary choice for on-line food delivery service by providing highest quality natural and organic, hygienic multi-cuisine food thereby satisfying and delighting customers with the experience of well trained delivery professionals.
    • Our vision is to grow with our on-line customers in our targeted markets by providing quality & hygienic multi-cuisine food at affordable prices along with excellent on-time delivery while enhancing our customer’s food delivery choices and experience.

    WE PLEDGE TO GIVE BACK YOUR FUNDAMENTAL RIGHT OF KNOWING THE FOOD ON THE PLATTER FROM THE TIME YOU ORDER THEM TO THE TASTE YOU CRAVED FOR .
